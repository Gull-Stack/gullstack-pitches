Where Things Stand
We audited byrneorthodontics.com. Here's what we found.

Live HTTP headers, raw HTML, and on-page SEO signals — captured this week. No assumptions. Just the data.

The technical reality

  • 30 JavaScript files + 18 CSS files load on every page — every one is a render-blocking round-trip the browser has to wait for before showing content.
  • Zero lazy-loading — every image on the homepage downloads at full size on first paint, even the ones below the fold.
  • 4 of 11 images missing alt attributes — Google can't read them, screen readers can't either (ADA exposure).
  • TTFB ~1.0s — modern dental sites hit ~200ms. Slow servers bleed into every Core Web Vitals score Google measures.
  • WordPress + WP Engine + WPRocket cache — well-configured at the server, but choked at the theme. The theme is the bottleneck.

What's missing entirely

  • No live website chat. Patients who want to ask "do you take my insurance?" at 9pm have no way to do it.
  • No embedded online scheduler. Booking the free consult means calling during business hours and hoping someone picks up.
  • No pay-by-text. Every balance is a paper statement, a phone call, or a portal login the patient has to remember.
  • No AI search optimization. When a Saratoga parent asks ChatGPT "best orthodontist for Invisalign near me," the answer doesn't include you yet — but it could.
