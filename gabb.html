The StoryBrand Problem
Gabb's website fails the Donald Miller framework test.

You've built a life-saving product, but your website positions Gabb as the hero instead of the parent. This fundamental messaging mistake costs conversions every day.

How Gabb Currently Positions Itself (WRONG)

  • "Connection. Safe and Simple." — Generic tagline that could describe any tech product
  • Gabb is the hero — "We protect," "We keep kids safe," "Our mission"
  • Product-focused CTAs — "Shop Now" instead of "Protect Your Child"
  • No clear villain — Dangerous tech and social media predators aren't positioned as the enemy
  • Benefits buried — The "Why Gabb" page is 3 clicks deep instead of homepage hero
  • No stakes section — What happens if parents DON'T act?
  • Tells, doesn't show — Claims safety instead of showing transformation stories

StoryBrand Framework (How It Should Be)

  • Hero = Parent — "You want to keep your child safe in a digital world"
  • Villain = Dangerous Tech — Social media predators, addiction, inappropriate content
  • Guide = Gabb — "We understand your fears and have the solution"
  • Plan = 3 Simple Steps — Choose device → Activate plan → Child stays safe
  • Call to Action — "Protect Your Child Today"
  • Stakes = High — "Every day without Gabb is another day your child is exposed"
  • Success = Transformation — "Parent testimonials showing peace of mind"

AEO/SEO Audit
When parents ask ChatGPT "what's the safest phone for kids?" — Gabb isn't mentioned.

Your 300K families prove Gabb is the answer, but AI assistants can't see that expertise because of missing structured data.

Critical AI/Search Visibility Gaps

  • No /.well-known/brand-facts.json — Returns 404, invisible to AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini
  • Missing FAQPage schema — Have FAQ content, but search engines can't understand it
  • No LocalBusiness schema — Utah HQ and operations invisible to local search
  • Minimal Organization schema — 300K families, founding story, mission not structured for AI
  • No ItemList schema — Product lineup (Watch 3e, Phone 4, Phone 4 Pro) not properly marked up
  • No BreadcrumbList schema — Site navigation invisible to search crawlers
  • Missing ProductReview schema — Thousands of reviews not marked up for rich snippets

AEO Implementation Strategy

  • Brand Facts API — Make Gabb discoverable to AI assistants with structured company data
  • Q&A Content Optimization — Structure FAQ content to answer "safest phone for kids" queries directly
  • Product Schema Markup — Gabb Watch 3e, Phone 4, Phone 4 Pro with reviews, pricing, safety features
  • Safety Feature Schema — GPS tracking, no social media, approved contacts as searchable attributes
  • Parent Testimonial Schema — Structure reviews to show transformation stories
  • Educational Content Hub — "Digital Safety for Kids" content that positions Gabb as the expert
