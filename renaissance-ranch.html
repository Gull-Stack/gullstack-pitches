What AI Engines Need (And Can't Find)

  • No /brand-facts page — AI models can't verify your credentials, founding date, outcomes, or specialties
  • No /.well-known/brand-facts.json — The machine-readable identity file that AI agents use to verify businesses
  • No FAQPage schema — AI assistants can't extract structured Q&A from your site
  • No LocalBusiness schema per facility — Each of your 7 locations should have its own structured data
  • No AggregateRating schema — Your success rates and reviews aren't machine-readable
  • Only 1 Review schema — One testimonial marked up vs. the hundreds you likely have
  • No Answer Hub content — No guide pages structured for AI to quote directly
