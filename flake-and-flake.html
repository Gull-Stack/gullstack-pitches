Critical Issues We Found

  • "Confidence Legal" still on live pages — Your estate planning page says "Call Confidence Legal today" twice. Social media profiles still link to the old brand. This is a trust killer.
  • Only 11 total pages — Competing Henderson/Vegas law firms have 50-100+ pages. You're bringing a business card to a content war.
  • Only 2 blog posts — One has a URL typo (/estate-plannings/ with an extra "s"). For a firm competing in PI and estate planning, this is near-invisible to Google.
  • No dedicated Personal Injury page — PI is a massive keyword category. Your services page lumps everything together. Each practice area needs its own deep, authoritative page.
  • Zero local SEO pages — No Henderson, Las Vegas, or Boulder City landing pages. Local search is how 73% of legal clients find their attorney.
  • No LegalService or Attorney schema — Basic Yoast defaults only. Google and AI search engines can't properly categorize your expertise.
  • GoDaddy + Divi = slow and bloated — Page speed directly impacts search rankings and visitor trust.
  • No case results or settlement amounts — PI clients want to see numbers before they call. Your competitors show them.
  • Copyright says 2024 — Small detail, but signals the site isn't actively maintained.
