SEO Audit
What we found under the hood.

We ran a technical audit on threadwallets.com. The product schema is solid — but everything around it is leaving money on the table.

Critical Issues Found

  • Meta description has a typo — "Sho wallets" instead of "Shop wallets" in your Google snippet. Every searcher sees this.
  • No review schema on products — You use Okendo (great platform), but review data isn't being surfaced as rich snippets. That means no star ratings in Google results.
  • Empty social meta tags — Twitter site tag is blank, multiple sameAs entries are empty strings, OG image uses http instead of https
  • Collection pages have thin content — 50-word descriptions buried below the fold. No keyword-rich buying guides, no comparison content.
  • Blog is lifestyle-only — "The Loom" has playlists and artist spotlights but zero intent-driven SEO content targeting actual wallet search queries
  • Homepage title not optimized — "Stylish Wallets" vs high-volume terms like "slim wallets" or "minimalist wallets" (10x+ search volume)

What's Working Well

  • Product schema is solid — Product pages have proper structured data with pricing, availability, and return policy
  • BreadcrumbList schema — Proper breadcrumb markup on collection and product pages
  • Fast Shopify infrastructure — HTTP/2, Cloudflare CDN, proper caching headers
  • Strong brand identity — "Carry On" mission, patented elastic wallet, clear market positioning
  • Large product catalog — 131+ wallets across 5 categories gives you massive keyword surface area

Conversion Rate Optimization
Your site gets visitors. It doesn't convert enough of them.

Quick wins that compound into serious revenue growth — no redesign required.

Homepage Improvements

  • Add social proof above the fold — Review count badge, star rating, "50,000+ happy customers" type signal. Right now the hero is all brand, no proof.
  • Sharpen the value proposition — "Relentless Progression" is brand voice, not a conversion driver. Lead with what makes Thread different: patented, ultra-slim, $16 starting price.
  • Bundle prominence — Trifecta Bundles are buried. Feature them with savings callouts to increase AOV.

Collection & Product Pages

  • Collection buying guides — Rich content above the product grid: "Which Thread wallet is right for you?" with comparison cards
  • "Best For" badges — Tag products: "Best for Students," "Best for Travel," "Best Seller" — reduces decision fatigue
  • Wallet comparison tool — Interactive: Elastic vs Bifold vs Vertical — card capacity, dimensions, use case. Reduces bounce, increases confidence.
  • Exit-intent capture — "Find your perfect wallet" quiz or 10% off popup for first-time visitors. Every bounce without an email is a lost customer.
