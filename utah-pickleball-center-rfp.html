Project Overview

TC Enterprise is developing the Utah Pickleball Center, a 166,823 square foot mixed-use facility that will serve as a premier destination for pickleball in the Western United States.

The building will house four committed tenants with 100% of leasable space pre-leased:

Tenant Use Square Footage
PPA Tour Corporate Offices 20,000 sq ft
Utah Black Diamonds Team Offices 5,000 sq ft
Pickleball Central Flagship Retail Store 12,000 sq ft
SANC Investments Signature Pickler Club 132,000 sq ft
Total Leasable Area 166,823 sq ft

Lease & Tenant Summary

1. PPA Tour (Professional Pickleball Association)

Description: The PPA Tour is the premier professional pickleball tour in the United States, organizing and broadcasting professional pickleball tournaments nationwide. The PPA Tour features the world's top players and has secured major broadcast partnerships.

Use of Space: Corporate headquarters and administrative offices for tour operations, event planning, player relations, and media production.

Lease Terms:

  • Square Footage: 20,000 sq ft
  • Rate: $24.00/sq ft annually
  • Monthly Rent: $40,000 | Annual Rent: $480,000
  • Annual Escalation: 2.5%
  • Lease Term: 10 years with two 5-year renewal options

2. Utah Black Diamonds

Description: The Utah Black Diamonds is a professional pickleball team competing in Major League Pickleball (MLP), representing Utah in the nation's premier team pickleball competition.

Use of Space: Team offices for player management, training coordination, marketing operations, and administration.

Lease Terms:

  • Square Footage: 5,000 sq ft
  • Rate: $24.00/sq ft annually
  • Monthly Rent: $10,000 | Annual Rent: $120,000
  • Annual Escalation: 2.5%
  • Lease Term: 10 years with two 5-year renewal options

3. Pickleball Central

Description: Pickleball Central is the largest dedicated pickleball retailer in the United States, offering paddles, equipment, apparel, and accessories.

Use of Space: Flagship retail store and pro shop serving facility members, tournament participants, and the general public.

Lease Terms:

  • Square Footage: 12,000 sq ft
  • Rate: $10.00/sq ft annually
  • Monthly Rent: $10,000 | Annual Rent: $120,000
  • Annual Escalation: 2.5%
  • Lease Term: 10 years with two 5-year renewal options

4. SANC Investments (Pickler Franchisee — Signature Club)

Description: SANC Investments is the franchisee and operator of a Signature Pickler club. The Pickler is a premium pickleball club franchise offering memberships, programming, leagues, tournaments, fitness facilities, and food & beverage amenities.

Use of Space: Full-service Pickler signature club including indoor pickleball courts, fitness center, café/lounge, member amenities, and event/tournament hosting. SANC Investments will also serve as building operator.

Lease Terms:

  • Square Footage: 132,000 sq ft
  • Rate: $16.25/sq ft annually
  • Monthly Rent: $178,750 | Annual Rent: $2,145,000
  • Annual Escalation: 2.5%
  • Lease Term: 10 years with two 5-year renewal options
