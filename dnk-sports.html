The Conversion Audit
Great product. Terrible online experience.

The Bainbridge 1 might be perfectly engineered for pickleball, but your Shopify store makes it nearly impossible for customers to trust, understand, or buy.

What's Killing Your Conversions

  • Zero social proof — No reviews visible, no testimonials, no "as seen in" badges for a $100+ unknown brand
  • Hidden pricing — No price on homepage, requires click-through. Creates friction when price should sell
  • Weak value proposition — "Finally, a shoe made for pickleball" doesn't address ankle injuries, court grip, lateral support
  • No urgency or scarcity — Nothing pushes visitors from browsing to buying today
  • Buried product video — Video exists but hidden behind clicks. Shoe brands live or die by visuals
  • Missing trust signals — No sizing guide, returns policy unclear, no FAQ addressing buyer concerns
  • No educational content — Missing "why pickleball shoes matter" content to justify premium pricing
  • Generic CTAs — "Shop the look" sounds like apparel, not performance footwear

Quick Wins That Convert More Browsers

  • Add review system — Even 10-20 authentic reviews boost conversion 15-20%
  • Front-load your price — If competitive, show it prominently. If premium, justify the value
  • Rewrite hero with pain points — Focus on ankle injuries from running shoes, not "ruined shoes"
  • Embed video in hero — Autoplay muted product video showing court performance
  • Add sizing guide + returns policy — Remove purchase anxiety for high-priced footwear
  • Create urgency — Limited production run, pre-order deadline, anything time-sensitive
  • Build "Why Pickleball Shoes?" page — Educational content that justifies premium pricing
  • Optimize for mobile — 60%+ of traffic shops on phones, experience must be flawless

What You Have Going For You

  • Shopify foundation — Solid e-commerce platform, just needs optimization
  • Great product positioning — "The Bainbridge 1" sounds premium and specific
  • Focused product line — Single SKU makes optimization easier than complex catalogs
  • Niche specialization — Pickleball-specific gives you positioning advantage over general athletic brands
  • Clean visual design — Good foundation, just needs conversion optimization
