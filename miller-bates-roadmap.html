Decisions we need from your team.

Authentication Approach

  • Option A: Email + password (traditional, simple)
  • Option B: SSO — Sign in with Google / Sign in with Apple
  • Option C: Both — email/password + SSO options
  • We can also add two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security on financial data
Recommendation: Option C (both) — gives investors flexibility, SSO reduces password fatigue

Marketing Strategy
Attract more borrowers, brokers, and investors.

Taylor's priority: deal flow is the oil in the engine. Here's how the new website drives it.

Borrower & Broker Acquisition (Priority #1)

  • Targeted landing pages for hard money loan seekers in Utah
  • Broker-specific content and application flow
  • Google Ads optimization — you're already running ads, let's make them convert better
  • Retargeting — visitors from ads get followed across the web back to millerbates.com
  • SEO targeting: "hard money lenders utah" (#14 → top 5), "private money loans utah" (#4 → #1)

Investor Attraction

  • Dedicated investor pages with clear value proposition
  • Trust signals — track record, portfolio stats, $46M+ AUM
  • Streamlined registration flow from marketing site to portal
  • Content strategy — market insights, project case studies, investor education

SEO & AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

  • Schema markup: Organization, FinancialService, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList
  • Brand-facts page + /.well-known/brand-facts.json for AI crawlers
  • Goal: When someone asks ChatGPT "best hard money lender in Utah" — Miller Bates is the answer
  • Key target: "hard money lenders utah" (170 searches/mo, currently #14)

Google Ads + Retargeting Integration

  • Connect website analytics to your existing Google Ads campaigns
  • Retarget every visitor — borrowers who didn't apply, investors who didn't register
  • Refine ad targeting — use website data to reduce wasted spend
  • Conversion tracking — know exactly which ads produce applications
